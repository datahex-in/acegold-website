Top 5 Reasons For YOU To Save Money In Gold

(Est Reading Time - 5 mins )

Saving in gold is not something new for Indians. It is an emotion for us. You must have seen  your parents buying gold with their saved money to gift you on the occasion of your marriage. For them, it is like passing on the legacy. The real idea behind this is to protect you from bad times and give you the gift of security.

Gold has always been a popular precious metal in India. Because of its high demand and worth, its value rises over time, providing good returns and investment value in the long run.

You will be surprised but according to the World Gold Council (WGC) India's gold demand reached 191.7 tonnes in July-September 2022 showing an annual growth of 14%. Isn't that huge?

But it's not just about the numbers, gold offers a unique combination of stability, diversification, and long-term growth potential that makes it an excellent addition to any savings plan. 

So let’s get started. 

Why Should You Save / Invest In Gold Today?


1. You Can Hedge Against Inflation By Saving In Gold

As per a report by WGC, For every 1% increase in Inflation, gold demand In India increases by 2.6% which results in increased gold rates. So if you buy gold now, it will protect you against inflation in future as the value of your gold will increase. This makes it an attractive investment option for you to protect your wealth in the long term.

2. Saving Money In Gold Is Effective Tool For Portfolio Diversification

You must have heard from personal finance gurus that Diversification is the key to any successful investment strategy. Well, gold can play a crucial role in diversifying your portfolio. Holding a portion of your investments in gold can help reduce overall risk. It is because gold performs differently than other asset classes, such as stocks and bonds. You know what? A study by the World Gold Council found that during the 2008 financial crisis, the price of gold increased by 17% while global equity markets fell by 37%. So imagine the power it holds and how effectively it manages the overall risk and diversifies your portfolio.

3. It Is a Great Store of Value To Preserve Your Wealth

Gold has been used as a store of value for centuries, and its value has remained relatively stable over time. This makes it a reliable option for long-term investors looking to preserve their wealth.

4. If You Invest In Gold Today, It Will Protect You From Economic Downturns Tomorrow

Economic downturns are inevitable, but you can protect your wealth by investing in assets that hold up well during times of market volatility. Gold has historically been a safe haven during economic downturns, as its value tends to hold up well during times of market volatility. This is because investors tend to flock to gold as a safe haven during times of uncertainty, which can drive up its price. It is a stable investment option because it is less volatile as compared to other assets. 

Talking about other assets, if you want a detailed comparison of gold with other assets, check out the blog about why choose gold as an investment/savings. 

5. It Can Also Hedge Against Currency Fluctuations

Gold can be a hedge against currency fluctuations, as its value is not tied to any specific currency. It can be beneficial for investors concerned about their currency's stability.

Now, let's get back to the reasons why you should save in digital gold.

1. Security: It is Always With You Wherever You Go

Storing physical gold can be risky as it can be stolen or lost, whereas digital gold can be stored in a secure digital wallet, providing an added layer of security.

2. Cost-effectiveness: You Don't Need To Bear High Locker Charges

3. Liquidity: You Can Easily Buy and Sell Gold When It’s Digital

Gold is a highly liquid asset, meaning it can easily be bought and sold on the market. It makes it a convenient investment option for those who may need to liquidate their assets quickly.
